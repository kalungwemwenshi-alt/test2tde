About Me

Kalungwe Mwenshi, The Dot-com Entrepreneur, is a top-tier sales and marketing coach and business consultant. He empowers business owners and entrepreneurs to close bigger deals, build stronger brands, and scale their ventures through practical strategies that actually work.

I’m Kalungwe Mwenshi, a global speaker, author, and entrepreneur dedicated to helping people unlock influence and build lasting income in the digital age. With years of experience in business, sales, and personal development, I’ve built platforms that empower entrepreneurs and creators to thrive.

A high-achieving graduate, Coach and Trainer, Kalungwe combines academic excellence with professional mastery, holding certifications in sales, negotiation, pitching, brand management, programming, Data Analysis and business automation.

My passion is transforming ideas into impact — guiding others to find clarity, take action, and create wealth through influence. This book “YOU ARE THE KEY” is a reflection of that journey.
